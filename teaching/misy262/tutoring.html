MISY262 Tutoring Program

Lerner's MISY262 Tutoring Program, which is generously funded by alumni, will be available as an additional support to MISY262 students. The tutoring program support students beyond the classroom and intstructor office hours in several ways such as answering student questions, offering help with homework and preparation for assessments.

In Fall 2020, tutoring program will be completely online via Zoom and Slack. Zoom links to attend live tutoring sessions will be available on this site. We will also have a Slack channel where students will be able to chat with tutors. Instructors of MISY262 will provide the link to their sections to join the Slack channel.

Tutoring hours will be consistent week to week, but any last minute changes will be posted here and on Slack.
