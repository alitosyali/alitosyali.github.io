Ali Tosyali

Assistant Professor of Management Information Systems
Rochester Institute of Technology

Hello! I am an assistant professor in the Department of MIS, Marketing, and Analytics in the Saunders College of Business at Rochester Institute of Technology. Before that, I was an assistant professor in the Department of Accounting and MIS in the Alfred Lerner College of Business and Economics at the University of Delaware. I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Industrial & Systems Engineering from Rutgers University, and my B.S. in Business Administration and Management from Turkish Military Academy.

My research focuses on developing new methodologies for various data-driven business problems. My interests lie broadly in network science, machine learning, optimization, and complex systems driven by applications in financial services analytics, social network analysis, opinion mining and analysis, and business analytics.

Besides research and teaching, I like playing racquetball, playing baglama (a folkloric instrument), and snowboarding. But I am especially passionate about playing soccer.

See also my CV and departmental website.
