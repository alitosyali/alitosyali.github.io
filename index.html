Ali Tosyali

Assistant Professor of Management Information Systems
University of Delaware

Hello! I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Accounting & Management Information Systems at the University of Delaware. Before that, I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Industrial & Systems Engineering from Rutgers University, and my B.S. in Business Administration and Management from Turkish Military Academy. During my Ph.D. education, I also worked as a Lecturer at Rutgers Business School, where I taught graduate and undergraduate level courses in management science and information systems.

Besides teaching and research, I like playing racquetball, playing baglama (a folkloric instrument), and snowboarding. But I am especially passionate about playing soccer.
